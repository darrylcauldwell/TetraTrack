Welcome to the only training app that actually gets tetrathlon and eventing. We know you're not just a runner who occasionally sits on a horse, or a swimmer who dabbles in target practice. You're gloriously, chaotically multi-talented — and finally, there's an app for that.

APPLE WATCH FIRST

Start every session from your wrist. Riding, running, swimming, walking, shooting — tap and go. Big buttons for gloved fingers, live heart rate, discipline-specific metrics. No phone fishing required.

Your iPhone is the brains of the operation — enriching every session with detailed metrics, insights, route maps, and coaching after you're done. Watch captures, iPhone reviews.

FIVE DISCIPLINES, ONE SLIGHTLY OBSESSIVE APP

RIDING — Start from Watch with ride, dressage, or showjumping modes. iPhone enriches with HR zones, route, elevation, and weather. Annotate with horse, scores, and notes. Manage multiple horses with profiles and training histories.

RUNNING — Pace and cadence tracked live on Watch. iPhone shows splits, HR analysis, and session insights. Pre-race 1500m warmup plan included.

SWIMMING — Lap count and stroke detection on Watch. iPhone reviews pace, SWOLF, and lap-by-lap breakdowns.

WALKING — Steps per minute and distance on Watch. iPhone enriches with route and heart rate data.

SHOOTING — Steadiness and heart rate tracked live on Watch. Scan targets with iPhone camera for shot analysis — mark holes, see heatmaps, track groupings.

TRAINING DRILLS

Off-horse and off-range drills to build the movement patterns that transfer across all disciplines. Core stability, balance board, posting rhythm, hip mobility, steady hold, dry fire, reaction training, and more. Real-time motion feedback scores your form as you go.

SCHOOLING LIBRARY

Flatwork, polework, and groundwork exercises with step-by-step instructions. Category-organised, searchable, and always available — no internet needed.

COMPETITION SEASON

Track your Pony Club calendar with countdown timers and competition day scoring. Record riding penalties, shooting points, running times, and swimming distances all in one place.

LIVE SHARING (KEEP MUM HAPPY)

Riding alone doesn't mean being alone. Share your live location so family can see exactly where you are and panic slightly less.

SESSION HISTORY

Every session — Watch workouts, drills, and external activities — in one colour-coded timeline. Discipline-specific metrics, coaching insights, and training load trends.

PRIVACY FIRST

Your data stays on your devices. Apple Intelligence processes locally. We're not selling your canter statistics to anyone. Promise.
